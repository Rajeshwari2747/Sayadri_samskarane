
# SAHYADRI SAMSKARANE

## Abstract

Sahyadri Samskarane is a mobile application created to protect and promote our culture, traditions, festivals, and historical places. The app helps people learn about their heritage through their smartphones. It provides cultural information, event updates, and AI-based suggestions. The main purpose of this project is to make cultural knowledge easily available to everyone, especially students and young people.

## Introduction

Nowadays, many people are busy with technology and social media, so they know less about their culture and traditions. Because of this, many cultural values are slowly being forgotten. Sahyadri Samskarane is developed to solve this problem. It is a simple Android application that helps users learn about local traditions, festivals, history, and cultural events in an easy way.

## Problem Statement

Many cultural traditions and customs are disappearing because people are not aware of them. There is no single platform where users can find all cultural information easily. Young people are showing less interest in learning about heritage and traditions. Therefore, a digital platform is needed to preserve and share cultural information.

## Objectives

* To preserve culture and traditions in digital form.
* To provide cultural information to users.
* To increase awareness about heritage among young people.
* To encourage participation in cultural events.
* To use AI technology for better user experience.
* To develop a simple and user-friendly Android application.

## Learning Outcomes

Through this project, we learned:

* Android app development using Android Studio.
* Firebase database and authentication.
* AI integration in mobile applications.
* MVVM architecture concepts.
* Mobile app design and development.
* The importance of preserving cultural heritage using technology.

## Conclusion

Sahyadri Samskarane is a useful application that helps people learn and understand their culture and traditions. It combines modern technology with cultural preservation. The project creates awareness among users and helps keep cultural heritage alive for future generations.
