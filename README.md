# SAHYADRI SAMSKARANE

## ABSTRACT

Sahyadri Samskarane is an Android-based mobile application developed to preserve, promote, and spread awareness about cultural heritage, traditions, festivals, and historical places. In today's digital world, people spend most of their time using smartphones and social media platforms. As a result, knowledge about local culture, customs, and traditional values is gradually decreasing, especially among younger generations. This project aims to bridge the gap between technology and culture by providing a digital platform where users can easily access cultural information.

The application offers details about festivals, historical monuments, traditions, folk arts, and cultural events. Users can explore cultural content through a simple and user-friendly interface. The application also integrates Artificial Intelligence (AI) to provide personalized recommendations based on user interests. Firebase is used for secure authentication and cloud-based data storage.

The main goal of Sahyadri Samskarane is to make cultural knowledge accessible to everyone, encourage participation in cultural activities, and ensure that valuable traditions are preserved for future generations. By combining modern technology with cultural preservation, the application contributes to the protection of heritage and promotes cultural awareness in society.

# INTRODUCTION

Culture is the identity of a community, state, or nation. It includes traditions, customs, festivals, languages, historical monuments, art forms, and values passed down through generations. Cultural heritage plays a significant role in shaping society and maintaining a connection with the past.

In recent years, rapid technological development and modernization have changed people's lifestyles. Most individuals, especially young people, are more interested in digital entertainment and social media than learning about their culture and traditions. As a result, many valuable customs, local stories, and traditional practices are slowly disappearing.

Sahyadri Samskarane was developed to address this challenge. The application serves as a digital platform that allows users to learn about cultural heritage using their smartphones. It provides information about historical places, traditional festivals, local customs, and cultural events in a convenient and interactive manner.

The project utilizes Android Studio for application development, Firebase for database management and user authentication, and AI technologies for intelligent recommendations. The application aims to increase cultural awareness, encourage learning, and make heritage information available to users anytime and anywhere.

# PROBLEM STATEMENT

Culture and traditions are important parts of society, but many people today have limited knowledge about them. Several factors contribute to this issue:

1. Lack of awareness among young generations.
2. Limited access to reliable cultural information.
3. Absence of a centralized platform containing cultural resources.
4. Decreasing participation in cultural events and celebrations.
5. Influence of modernization leading to reduced interest in traditional practices.
6. Risk of losing historical and cultural knowledge over time.

Most cultural information is scattered across books, websites, and local sources, making it difficult for users to find accurate information in one place. Therefore, there is a need for a modern digital solution that preserves and shares cultural heritage effectively.

Sahyadri Samskarane addresses this problem by providing a centralized mobile application where users can access cultural information, explore traditions, and receive updates about cultural events.
